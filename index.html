Shore Club

A 200 foot tall oceanfront tower, with 49 new residences, Shore Club is the latest addition to the iconic Miami Beach. Designed by Robert A.M. Stern Architects and developed by Witkoff and Monroe Capital, Shore Club is set to be an exciting future centrepiece of South Beach.

The Boundary created 38 CGIs and Cinemagraphs for the marketing launch of the newest Miami Beach tower standing at 18 floors tall.

The historic Shore Club was originally designed by Albert Anis and completed in 1949 along Miami Beach’s famed Collins Avenue. Witkoff and Monroe Capital are now in the process of redeveloping and reinventing the tower as a boutique 5-star resort by Auberge Resorts Collection.

1 8

Influenced by its surroundings, the rounded shape of the tower serves as a reminder of the ocean waves and nautical environment. The interior of the residences pay homage with subtle references to materials and shapes of classic yachts, including the use of woods such as mahogany and oak, with accents of light metalwork.

Residents will have access to resort style amenities from private rooftop pools, exclusive gardens, fitness centres, library, lounge and private cabanas, all provided and host for by Auberge Resorts Collections tailored and intimate service.
